FAQ

What is the S/U option?

NTU allows you to mark certain courses as Satisfactory/Unsatisfactory. S grades don't affect your GPA but count toward graduation credits.

How is CGPA calculated?

CGPA = Sum of (Grade Points Ã— Credits) / Total Graded Credits (excluding S/U courses).

When should I use the target GPA calculator?

Use it to plan what grades you need in remaining courses to achieve your desired CGPA for honours classification or exchange programs.

Is my data saved?

Yes, all data is saved locally in your browser. No information is sent to servers. Use Export to create backups.
