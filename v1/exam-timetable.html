FAQ

Where do I find my official exam schedule?

Check NTU Student Portal > Academics > Examination Timetable or STARS system.

What if I have an exam conflict?

Exam conflicts are rare but serious. Immediately contact both course coordinators and the Exam Unit.

Can I share my timetable?

Yes! Export to PDF and share, or send the .ics file for others to import to their calendars.

How do I import to Google Calendar?

Click "Export to Calendar", download the .ics file, then in Google Calendar: Settings > Import & Export > Select file.
