Your Complete NTU Student Companion

NTU Info is a student-built portal that brings essential tools and guides into one place.

What you can do here

  • CGPA Calculator: Track courses by semester and compute your cumulative CGPA.
  • Clubs & Societies Explorer: Search and filter clubs by category, time commitment, and location.
  • Airport Arrival Guide: Step-by-step onboarding for international students arriving at Changi.
  • Exam Timetable: Plan exams and avoid clashes (Academics tab).

How to use

  1. Use the banner tabs to jump between Academics, Tools, Guides, and Campus Life.
  2. On each tool page, use filters and inputs; changes apply instantly.
  3. Your calculator data is stored locally in your browser (no server upload).

FAQ

Does NTU Info collect my data?

No. Tools like the CGPA calculator store data locally in your browser only.

Is this an official NTU site?

No. This is a student-led initiative and not officially affiliated with NTU administration.
